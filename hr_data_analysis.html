(1) Salary:

  • Average = $69,020.68
  • Median = $62,810.00

Median salary is a more realistic salary for most employees at this company due the median being significantly lower ($6,210.68) than the average. In other words, there are some outliers on the top end of the salaries at this company that skew the average salary.

(2) Engagement:

  • Average: 4.11 out of 5.00
  • Median: 4.28 out of 5.00
  • Standard Deviation: 0.79

Employee engagement looks relatively great at this company. Both average and median are close together and both over 4.00 on a 5.00 scale. Also, standard deviation is low at 0.79 which tells me the extremes are kept in check (data isn't super spread out).
